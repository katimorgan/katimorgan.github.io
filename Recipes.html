Mawmaw Snookie's Famous Nine Day Pickles

First Day: Slice 1 gallon of cucumbers in a glass gallon jar; cover with 1 cup of salt and enough cold water to cover. Let stand for 4 days.

Fifth Day: Drain salt water and cover with boiling water and 1 tablespoon of Alum.

Sixth Day: Drain liquid and reheat. Pour over cucumbers.

Seventh Day: Drain liquid and mix the following: 3 cup of vinegars, 6 cups of sugar, 3 teaspoon of pickling spices.
Bring ingredients to a boil and pour over cucumbers.

Eighth Day: Drain and reheat liquid, add 1 cup of sugar to the mix. Once heated pour over cucumbers.

Ninth Day: Reheat liquid. Pack pickles in sterilized jars and pour hot liquid over pickles and seal the jars.

 

Mrs. Carolyn's Pecan Pie

Ingredients:

3 eggs, beaten
1 cup of Karo syrup
1/8 tsp. of salt
1 tsp. of vanilla
1 cup of sugar
2/3 cup chopped pecans
1 frozen pie shell

Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Mix all ingredients together, adding pecans last. Pour into pie shell. Bake in hot oven for 10 minutes, then reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue baking until center of filling is set. About 30 minutes.
